Personal profile

Wang Zhuo, Assistant Professor, Special Associate Researcher, and Master's Supervisor in the School of Computer Science.
Obtained a Ph.D. in Engineering from the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University in 2023, supervised by Professor Jianyong Wang (IEEE Fellow, CAAI Fellow). From 2023 to 2024, worked as a Senior Algorithm Engineer (Ant Star) at Ant Group, and later joined Beijing Institute of Technology.
Selected for the A-series High-Level Talent Recruitment Program at Beijing Institute of Technology, published 9 papers in international top conferences and journals in fields such as data mining, machine learning, and healthcare, including 4 papers as the first author in top international conferences and journals like TPAMI, NeurIPS, and AAAI, and one invention patent application has been authorized.
The research group has long-standing academic collaborations with top domestic and international research teams such as Harvard University, Cornell University, the University of Sydney, the University of California, Los Angeles, the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, Tsinghua University, and the Institute of Computing Technology of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. It has established long-term university-industry partnerships with Huawei Central Research Institute, Li Auto, and State Grid. The group admits multiple doctoral and master's students annually and welcomes students interested in research related to artificial intelligence and brain science.
