Personal profile

Wu Xia, Professor, Doctoral Supervisor, National Level Talent. In 2001, obtained a Bachelor's degree in Communication and Information Systems from Beijing Normal University, in 2004 obtained a Master's degree in Communication and Information Systems from Beijing Normal University, and in 2008 obtained a Doctorate in Basic Psychology from Beijing Normal University.
Hosted over 10 key projects of the National Natural Science Foundation of China and key research and development programs of the Ministry of Science and Technology, and received awards such as the first prize of the Wu Wenjun Artificial Intelligence Natural Science Award, the second prize of the Ministry of Education Natural Science Award, and the Mao Yisheng Beijing Young Science and Technology Award.
Over the past five years, more than a hundred papers have been published in international journals and conferences such as IEEE TNNLS, IEEE TKDE, IEEE TAFFC, IEEE JBHI, NeuroImage, MedIA, AAAI, IPMI, and MICCAI.
Each year, we recruit 3 Ph.D. students and 3 to 4 Master's students. We welcome students interested in research on artificial intelligence, brain science, and related fields to join the "Brain-Computer Interface and Artificial Brain Intelligence Research Center."
