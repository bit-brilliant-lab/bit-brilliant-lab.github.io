Admission Information

Beijing Institute of Technology School of Computer Science and Technology Brain-Computer Interface and Brain-like Intelligence Research Center enrolls many doctoral and master's students every year, welcome interested students to contact us!

This laboratory regularly recruits postdoctoral researchers in the field of brain-computer interface and brain-like intelligence, as well as postdoctoral researchers jointly cultivated by universities and enterprises. Outstanding applicants can be recommended to the university for the Xuteli postdoctoral position and international exchange program.

In addition, the center long-term recruits on-campus and off-campus interns, those with the intention to further study and pursue a master's or doctoral degree, with a solid foundation in deep learning, neuroscience, and competitive experience are preferred! The center has sufficient research funds and owns multiple Huawei Ascend 910 series high-performance GPU servers and NVIDIA L40S, A100, and H100 high-performance GPU computing clusters, looking forward to your joining!
