Introduction

Beijing Institute of Technology's Brain-Computer Interface and Brain-like Intelligence Laboratory focuses on national major needs, revolves around artificial intelligence and neuroscience, and is committed to applying artificial intelligence technology theories and methods to explore human brain characteristics and cognitive function brain mechanisms. At the same time, it benefits from insights and inspirations gained from neuroscience research, developing new artificial intelligence theories and methods, and empowering brain-computer interface technology in industrial manufacturing, medical care, and military affairs.

The research group regularly carries out academic cooperation with top international and domestic research teams such as Harvard University, Cornell University, University of Sydney, University of California, Los Angeles, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, Tsinghua University, and the Institute of Computing Technology of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. The research group annually enrolls several doctoral and master's students, and welcomes students interested in research related to artificial intelligence and neuroscience to join.
